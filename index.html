Quadratic Equation Solver

A quadratic equation is an algebraic expression of the second degree in x. The quadratic equation in its standard form is ax2 + bx + c = 0, where a, b are the coefficients, c is the constant term, and x is the variable

Since the variable x is of the second degree, there are two roots or answer for this quadratic equation. The roots of the quadratic equation can be found by either solving by factorizing or through the use of a formula
